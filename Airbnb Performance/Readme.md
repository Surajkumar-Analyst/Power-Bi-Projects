## ✨ Project Snapshot

This project transforms Airbnb's historical data into an interactive **3-page business intelligence dashboard** built in Power BI.

The dashboard focuses on understanding:

📈 Platform Growth

🌍 Market Share by City

⭐ Customer Ratings

🏠 Room Type Preferences

📝 Review Behavior

🔒 Host Trust & Verification

🌦️ Seasonal Trends

Rather than predicting the future, the goal is to uncover the story hidden in Airbnb's historical data and provide actionable business insights.

# 🎯 Business Questions Solved

### 🌍 Which cities dominate Airbnb's market?

Paris, New York, and Sydney contribute nearly half of total listings and reviews.

### 💰 Which room types generate higher value?

Entire Place listings consistently command premium prices.

### ⭐ Which cities provide the best guest experience?

Mexico City and Rio de Janeiro achieved the highest ratings.

### 🔒 Can hosts be trusted?

More than two-thirds of Airbnb hosts are fully verified.

### 📝 How often do guests leave reviews?

98.5% of users wrote only 3 reviews or fewer.

# 💡 Key Insights

🔥 Airbnb experienced explosive growth between 2012–2016.

🔥 Paris remains the strongest market in terms of listings and reviews.

🔥 Entire Place accommodations dominate pricing.

🔥 Review activity is highly concentrated among low-frequency reviewers.

🔥 Trust verification is a major factor in host credibility.

🔥 Seasonal demand patterns vary significantly across cities.
