# 🍕 Domino's Pizza Sales Dashboard

> An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze Domino's Pizza sales performance, customer ordering behavior, product popularity, and revenue trends. The dashboard enables stakeholders to identify top-selling pizzas, peak ordering periods, and sales patterns for data-driven business decisions.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Domino's Pizza Sales Dashboard** provides a comprehensive analysis of pizza sales data through interactive visualizations and KPI tracking. It helps business managers, analysts, and restaurant operators understand customer purchasing behavior, monitor revenue performance, and optimize operational efficiency.

The dashboard transforms raw sales data into actionable insights by highlighting sales trends, order distribution, product performance, and peak business hours.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

* 📊 **Power BI Desktop** – Main data visualization platform used for report creation.
* 📂 **Power Query** – Data cleaning, transformation, and preparation.
* 🧠 **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Used for KPIs, calculated measures, and dynamic analytics.
* 🔗 **Data Modeling** – Relationships created between sales, orders, and product datasets.
* 📈 **Interactive Visualizations** – Charts, KPI cards, slicers, and treemaps for user-driven exploration.
* 📁 **File Format** – `.pbix` for development and `.png` for dashboard preview.

---

## 📂 Data Source

**Source:** Domino's Pizza Sales Dataset

The dataset contains transactional sales information including:

* Order ID
* Order Date & Time
* Pizza Name
* Pizza Category
* Quantity Sold
* Unit Price
* Total Sales
* Order Volume
* Weekday Information

The data enables detailed analysis of customer purchasing behavior and business performance.

# ✨ Dashboard Features & Highlights

## 🚨 Business Problem

Restaurant chains generate thousands of orders daily, making it difficult to manually identify:

* Which pizzas generate the highest revenue?
* What are the busiest ordering hours?
* Which days receive the most orders?
* How much revenue is being generated overall?
* Which products should receive more marketing focus?

Without proper analytics, businesses may miss opportunities to improve sales and customer satisfaction.

---

## 🎯 Goal of the Dashboard

The dashboard aims to:

* Monitor overall business performance.
* Track sales and order KPIs.
* Identify top-performing pizza products.
* Analyze customer ordering patterns.
* Discover peak sales periods.
* Support data-driven marketing and operational decisions.

# 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

**$818K**

Measures total revenue generated from all pizza orders.

### 🛒 Total Orders

**21K**

**50K**

Tracks the total pizzas sold across all categories.

**$38**

# 📈 Walkthrough of Key Visuals

## ⏰ Orders by Hour

Line chart showing order volume throughout the day.

### Insights:

* Peak ordering occurs during afternoon and evening hours.
* Lower order volume during early morning and late-night periods.
* Helps optimize staffing and delivery operations.

---

## 🌞 Orders by Time Period

Donut chart categorizing orders into:

* Afternoon (46%)
* Evening (39%)
* Night (9%)
* Other periods

### Insights:

* Afternoon contributes the largest share of orders.
* Evening remains a strong revenue-driving period.

---

## 📅 Orders by Weekday

Bar chart comparing order volumes across weekdays.

### Insights:

* Friday records the highest number of orders.
* Weekend demand remains consistently strong.
* Useful for inventory planning and workforce scheduling.

---

## 🍕 Best-Selling Pizzas

Vertical bar chart displaying top revenue-generating pizzas.

### Top Performers:

* Thai Chicken Pizza
* Barbecue Chicken Pizza
* Classic Deluxe Pizza
* Hawaiian Pizza
* Pepperoni Pizza

### Insights:

* A small number of products contribute significantly to total revenue.
* Supports menu optimization and promotional campaigns.

---

## 🏆 Pizza Sales by Name

Horizontal bar chart ranking pizzas based on sales performance.

### Insights:

* Quickly identifies high-demand menu items.
* Helps focus marketing and upselling efforts.

---

## 📊 Sales Contribution by Day

Treemap visualization showing sales distribution across weekdays.

### Insights:

* Friday and Thursday contribute a major portion of revenue.
* Weekday performance comparison becomes visually intuitive.
